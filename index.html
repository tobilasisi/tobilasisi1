Research Summary

The ongoing shift in the requirements engineering domain toward large-scale, distributed, and collaborative environments offers numerous opportunities for supporting human intensive tasks through the use of recommendation technologies. My research is focused on human factors in RE recommender systems. The majority of my research applies machine learning and recommender technologies to automating requirements engineering processes, and evaluating the quality of user experience and users’ attitudes to the acceptance of recommender technology in RE.
